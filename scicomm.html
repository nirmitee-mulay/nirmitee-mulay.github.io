Science Communication & Art

Bridging science and visual storytelling

Beyond the lab, I'm passionate about making science accessible and engaging through visual communication. I work with both traditional mediums (watercolor, oil, acrylics, gouache) and digital tools (Photoshop, Procreate, Adobe Illustrator) to create scientific illustrations, infographics, and art that brings research to life.

Science Communication Experience

🎨 Designer & Illustrator - Neuromatch

📅 November 2020 - November 2021 🌍 International, Remote

Created visual content for Neuromatch, a global platform providing open computational neuroscience education. Designed infographics, course materials, and visual aids to help communicate complex neuroscience concepts to a diverse, international audience.

✏️ Designer & Illustrator - Cogito 137

📅 November 2020 - October 2021 📍 IISER Kolkata, India

🎓 Workshop Facilitator - Biology Week 2021

📅 October 10, 2021 🏛️ Royal Society of Biology

Conducted a workshop on Sci-Art, teaching participants how to communicate scientific concepts through visual art. Explored the intersection of artistic expression and scientific accuracy, demonstrating how illustration can make complex biology more engaging and understandable.
