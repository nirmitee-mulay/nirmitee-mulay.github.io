Nirmitee Mulay

Consciousness Researcher | PhD Student at BCBL

I'm a consciousness researcher exploring the boundaries between conscious and unconscious processing in the human brain. My work combines experimental psychophysics, fMRI neuroimaging, and computational approaches to understand visual perception, emotional experience, and metacognition. With an interdisciplinary background spanning biology, physics, and mathematics, I'm passionate about bridging experimental rigor with theoretical modeling and committed to open science practices.
